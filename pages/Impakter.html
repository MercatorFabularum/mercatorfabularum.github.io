"What is Impakter?"

A Magazine Website


Impakter is a magazine website based in London, UK. Founded by Michele Bonanno and Edward Kreiman, the goal of the company is connecting baby-boomers and millennials through current news happening throughout the world. They hire writers who are experts in a field to write and share what is happening in their field. Imapakter mainly focuses on issues that increase sustainability for the environment and society and have 17 specific sustainable goals. Through sharing and partnering with sustainable groups around the world, they hope to increase communication for change and increase the world moral awareness.




Impakter has 4 main branches of information to reach this goal. These are Culture, Style, Society, and Philanthropy. Daily, articles are written in each branch in hopes that through reading them, people's mindset grows into a more sustainable one.



In addition to writing articles, Impakter keeps a index that tracks the sustainability of the largest companies in the world in multiple sectors. Companies are given a letter rating that corresponds to the level of sustainability that the company hold. Letters A and B are considered sustainable companies, C and D are considered companies that are working on it, and F is for companies that are not trying to increase their sustainability at all. These ratings are determined using the index from the UN Sustainability Development Goals.
